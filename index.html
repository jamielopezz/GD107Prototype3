Ditch Fast Fashion, Save the Environment

Fast fashion is flashy, trendy, and easily accessible. With the click of a button, you can have 20 new items at your doorstep. But have you thought of how fast fashion impacts the environment? Fast fashion creates excessive waste, pollution, and does so much more harmful things to the environment. If you are ready to ditch this harmful way of shopping, shopping secondhand is the way to go. How do you start?

Shift Your Mindset

Nowadays, people are seeking the thrill of the instant satisfaction you feel when you purchase an item from a fast fashion site. Fast fashion also makes you feel like you constantly need to be wearing the new trend. Shifting your mindset to understand that you don't need a brand new piece of clothing to have style, will help you move away from fast fashion. Secondhand clothing has character, uniqueness, and most of all, you have full creativity to build your own style.

Your local thrift stores are home to so many treasures. Do a quick search on Google, "Thrift stores near me" and you'll get a list of all of your options. Grab a friend and take a trip! It may be intimidating seeing all of the racks to look through, but just remember, all of the hidden treasure comes from searching through every item. It is also satisfying and exciting when your hard work pays off and you find a piece you really like!
